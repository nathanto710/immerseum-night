Immerse into the Museum at Nights

Welcome Master Students!

What new aspects of life do you want to explore as a master student? Connect and experience new things at our event where you can connect with Australian First Nations culture.

Event Details

Venue: Powerhouse Museum Castle Hill

Time: August, Every Friday

Activities:

  • Engaging Artist Talks: Guest speakers Kay Lee Williams and Lucy Simpsons will discuss cultural preservation.
  • Interactive Workshops: Participate in a scarves dyeing class with Kay Lee Williams and a drawing workshop with Lucy Simpson. Your artwork will be showcased alongside the artists' work.
  • Networking and Socialising: Dedicated space for international students to share cultural experiences and make new connections.
  • Immersive Experiences: Guided tours of First Nations artifacts and exhibits, led by First Nations team curators.
  • Live Music: Performances by Gus Williams and Frank Yamma.
  • Food and Beverage: Enjoy indigenous-inspired cuisine and beverages.

Registration

Number of participants: 30 people

Note: 1st come 1st served basis. A waiting list will be available.
